enXross Program Guidelines

Updated: February 9, 2026 (JST)

This page summarizes participation expectations for cohort applicants, active teams, and ecosystem partners. Final acceptance and sponsorship terms are confirmed through direct review with the enXross team.

Cohort Participation

  • Teams are expected to actively build and test in the Tokyo Dome City ecosystem during the residency.
  • Weekly program sessions and milestone reviews are core parts of the program cadence.
  • Founders should share progress transparently and participate in cross-team feedback sessions.

Community Conduct

  • Respectful conduct in Discord, in person, and during partner engagements is required.
  • No harassment, discrimination, or abusive behavior is tolerated.
  • Confidential partner information should not be shared outside approved channels.

Sponsorship Alignment

  • Sponsorship tracks are exclusive per vertical (Blockchain, AI, XR).
  • Sponsors should provide technical and/or ecosystem support aligned to cohort needs.
  • Final scope, deliverables, and branding are handled in sponsor agreements.
